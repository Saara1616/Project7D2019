Depression

Depression is a state of mental illness. It is characterized by deep, long- lasting feelings of sadness or despair. Depression can change an individual's thinking/feelings and affects his/her social behavior and sense of physical well-being. Everyone experiences sadness and unhappiness at some point in their lives. Clinical Depression, however, is more intense and of longer duration than typical sadness or grief, which interferes with a person's ability to engage in daily activities. .
